Community Clean-Up Initiatives

We organize regular clean-up drives in Kibera to ensure a cleaner and healthier environment. These initiatives involve local residents and aim to reduce waste and improve living conditions.

Sanitary pads Giveaways

Our sanitary pad giveaway programs focus on providing essential hygiene products to young women and girls in the community, promoting health and dignity.

Youth Development Programs

We offer various programs aimed at empowering youth through education, training, and mentorship. These programs help young people develop valuable skills and pursue their goals.
