OUR MISSION

Tembea Kibera is dedicated to empowering the community of Kibera through various programs and initiatives. Our mission is to create sustainable development and improve the quality of life for residents.

OUR VISION

We envision a thriving Kibera where every resident has access to opportunities and resources to improve their lives. Our vision is to foster a strong, resilient community through collaboration and support.

OUR CORE VALUES

  2. Discipline
  3. Teamwork
  4. Diversity and Inclusion
  5. Intergrity
  6. Peaceful Environment

What We Do

Community Clean-up Initiatives

We organize regular clean-up drives in Kibera to ensure a cleaner and healthier environment. These initiatives involve local residents and aim to reduce waste and improve living conditions. We partner with local organizations and businesses to enhance our clean-up efforts, making our impact even greater.

Sanitary Pads Give-aways

Our sanitary pad giveaway programs focus on providing essential hygiene products to young women and girls in the community, promoting health and dignity. We organize regular events to distribute sanitary pads to those in need, ensuring access to essential hygiene products.

Youth Development Programs

We offer various programs aimed at empowering youth through education, training, and mentorship. These programs help young people develop valuable skills and pursue their goals.

Educational Workshops

Our educational workshops focus on menstrual hygiene management, providing information and resources to help individuals manage their health effectively. We also distribute sanitary products during these workshops.
