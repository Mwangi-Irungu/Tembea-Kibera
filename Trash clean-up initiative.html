Trash Clean-Up Initiatives

Community Clean-Up Drives

Our community clean-up drives involve local residents coming together to remove trash from streets, parks, and other public areas. These events are organized regularly to promote a cleaner and healthier environment in Kibera.

Partnerships with Local Organizations

We partner with local organizations and businesses to enhance our clean-up efforts. These partnerships help provide resources and support for our initiatives, making our impact even greater.
