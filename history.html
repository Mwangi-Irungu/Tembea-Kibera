Background

Tembea Kibera was founded with the mission to empower the Kibera community through various initiatives. Our journey started with small community outreach programs and has grown into a larger movement aimed at making a significant impact.

Milestones

Over the years, we have achieved numerous milestones, including partnerships with key organizations, successful community projects, and significant improvements in the lives of Kibera residents. Each milestone represents our commitment to making a positive difference.

Future Goals

Looking ahead, we aim to expand our programs, strengthen our partnerships, and continue our efforts to create sustainable change. Our vision for the future includes broader community engagement and increased support for our initiatives.
